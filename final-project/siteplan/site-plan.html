Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to get people to look around more outside by keeping track of their sightings of different animals. It also has a purose to educate by sharingfacts about the animals they are sighting!

Audience

Nature enthusiast, hikers, kids, pretty much anyone that lives in southeast idaho and loves nature and observing it.

Dynamic elements

I will use dynamic elements by creating a search bar that will find info that matches the user input. I will use DOM to find and call different parts in the html and filling the with info from a specific object in the object list
