WildTally

How It Works!

To play this games you gotta take a few steps!

  • Step 1 : Go outside
  • Step 2 : Look all around
  • Step 3 : Type descriptors or animal names in the search bar
  • Step 4 : hit enter and find the critter you spotted in the resluts
  • Step 5 : On your critter, hit the button that says "add tally"

About Me

I have always loved being outdoors. It brings me peace and when I see little creatures or flowers I don't normally see, it reminds me of all of Gods creations and that he has a grand plan for me! Finding and learning about different creations of His brings me joy and I wanted to make a webpage that gamifies the animal tracking to create "incentive" to be outside, observing nature more. I believe it is a necessary thing for our health, physical, spiritual, and mental, to be outside.
